Name:Monogatari

Intro:Stories of Japanese monsters.

Main character

  • Araragi Koyomi: The hero of this light novels. Help heroines away from monsters.
  • Oshino Shinobu: One of the heroine of this light novels. Vampire.
  • Senjougahara Hitagi: One of the heroine of this light novels. The girlfriend of Koyomi.
  • Hanekawa Tsubasa: Koyomi's classmate. Also the monitor of the class.
  • Hachikuji Mayoi: Ghost. But looks like the normal student in primary school.
