Name:Tiger X Dragon

Intro:A love story in high school.

Main character

  • Takasu ryuuji: The hero of this light novels. At the beginning of story likes Minori but finally fall in love with Taiga.
  • Aisaka Taiga: The heroine of this light novels. At the beginning of story likes Yuusaku but finally fall in love with ryuuji.
  • Kitamura Yuusaku: Ryuuji's best friend.
  • Kushieda Minori: Taiga's best friend.
